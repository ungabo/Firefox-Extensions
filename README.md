I'm just dumping some extensions here that I've made for my personal use.

1. The google images one is so you can look through google image results like a full screen gallery view like they had many years ago, instead of just a small image in the right sidebar.
2. The site muter is for those sites that you always have to mute when they play audio if you don't want audio coming from it.  You just add the sites and when you open them the tab will be muted automatically.
3. The timed blocker is for sites you spend too much time on.  You add a site and set how long you want to be allowed to use it for.  When the time runs out the site is blocked for 1 hour.

4. Use as you wish.  I won't likely be adding more features to the existing ones, but feel free to grab them and do what you want with them.  They are very barebones to just do the thing I need and not meant to be used commercially.

5. I may add more as I create them.
